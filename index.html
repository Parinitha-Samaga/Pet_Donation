Our Mission

At HappyPaws Foundation, we are dedicated to providing temporary homes for pets in need and finding them loving, permanent families. Your support helps us cover medical expenses, food, and shelter for these animals. Together, we can make a positive impact on their lives.

How Your Donation Helps

Every donation goes directly to:

  • Covering veterinary care and medical expenses
  • Providing food, shelter, and comfort for rescued pets
  • Funding adoption programs and community outreach
